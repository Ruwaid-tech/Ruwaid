Why Self Haven exists

We created this space to help teens gently step away from comparison culture and reconnect with the parts of themselves that feel brave, compassionate, and true.

Our mission

Offer free, stigma-free mental wellness tools designed with teen voices.

Inclusive design

Activities center diverse bodies, feelings, and experiences. We champion soft self-talk and moments of joy over perfection.

Privacy first

No accounts are required. Any information you save — like journal entries or favorites — stays securely on your device.

Supportive adults

Caregivers, teachers, and counselors can share Self Haven as a gentle resource for check-ins, conversation starters, and self-regulation.

Built with heart

The team behind Self Haven includes youth advisors and mental health educators dedicated to reducing the pressure of social media comparison.

Need urgent support?

If you or a friend need immediate help, please reach out to a trusted adult or local helpline right away.

  • United States: Text or call 988 for the 988 Lifeline.
  • Canada: Text 686868 for Kids Help Phone.
  • UK & Ireland: Call 116 123 for Samaritans.
  • Australia: Call 13 11 14 for Lifeline.
  • Other locations: Visit opencounseling.com for worldwide hotlines.
