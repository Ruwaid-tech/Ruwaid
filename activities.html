Guided mindfulness

Short practices to help your body feel grounded and safe.

4-6 breathing

Inhale softly for four counts, exhale for six to calm your nervous system.

  1. Sit comfortably, shoulders relaxed.
  2. Place a hand on your belly and inhale through your nose for four counts.
  3. Exhale through pursed lips for six counts, feeling your shoulders drop.
  4. Repeat for five rounds, imagining a wave releasing tension each time.

Self-kindness check-in

A quiet scan that swaps harsh inner dialogue for gentle care.

  1. Close your eyes and notice three sensations without judging them.
  2. Whisper a kind phrase: “My feelings are real, and they get space here.”
  3. Stretch or shake out any lingering tension.

Confidence visualization

Create a scene where you feel supported and capable.

  1. Picture a safe place — a beach, library, or anywhere you love.
  2. Notice colors, sounds, and how your body feels there.
  3. See your future self smiling back and thanking you for showing up.
